Club Sparkle

クラブスパークル

MLM / wellness brand; Las Vegas and Japan

Last verified: 2026-07-04

Short answer: Club Sparkle is a multi-level marketing (MLM) wellness brand. Nevada SOS records show US registrations at 3525 W Cheyenne Ave, Las Vegas. Marketing materials identify Anthony Diaz as Chairman (Anthony Diaz IV on public-facing content per archived website). The brand uses a network marketing structure documented in Nevada corporate filings. Separately from Sparkle KK, Club Sparkle's US registration connects to an MLM distribution model with multiple related Nevada entities.
